About

Total Possession Soccer was founded in 2013 and has seen rapid growth since its birth. TPS offers a variety of soccer and fitness programs for kids and adults. What separates TPS from competing soccer training companies is that our coaches are passionate about the beautiful game and understand that in order to get youth athletes to reach their full potential, you must first find ways to connect with them individually and as team.

Recreation Programs

Here at TPS we fully understand the importance of providing professional soccer training for recreation players. Our experienced training staff will work side by side with the parent coaches to offer professional soccer drills which ensures your athlete improves in technique, abilities and understanding of the game. We also understand how important a role administration plays in running an entire rec program. Our qualified Administration staff will handle everything from uniforms to field reservations. Our trainers follow a strict curriculum which gives our athletes the opportunity to learn all elements of the beautiful game which is crucial for their development as soccer players.

Team Training

TPS offers high energy and professional team training for club, travel and recreation programs. Our experienced and professional coaching staff will take the time to teach these athletes the technical and tactical parts of the game. Our coaches understand that connecting with kids is essential to helping them progress fully to the next level.

Game Day Coaching

Our professional coaches are all licensed and experienced in game day coaching for all ages and skill levels. We understand that parent coaches sacrifice a lot of time and energy to volunteer and we are happy to help out on the sidelines on game days and offer professional coaching tactics that will ensure your teams success.

Private Lessons

Private lessons are a great way to build your athletes confidence by focusing on speed and agility,footwork,dribbling ability and ball control. TPS offers one on one and group private lessons at the convenience of your own home. If you can not accommodate, then we can use one of our field locations to run the lesson. Our very competitive rates and professional training outmatches the competition and ensures your athlete will make it to the next level and continue to strive for greatness!

TPS ELITE

This six week summer program provides a fun and energetic atmosphere for our elite players to strive to compete at the top levels. TPS ELITE offers ten 2 hour training sessions where our athletes will train hard and learn to play at the next level. We play in two tournaments in July in which our teams get the chance to play against top ranked boys and girls teams throughout the country.

Winter Clinics

TPS offers winter clinics on Saturday mornings at the Ukrainian center in Whippany. This is an eight week program and begins the first week in January. Our winter clinics provide our athletes with an opportunity to work on their ball skills and technique which will help them in their upcoming seasons. Registration opens October 15th and we offer a discounted rate if registered by November 15th.

Personal Fitness Training for Youth and Adult Athletes

Whether you are a youth athlete that wants to improve on fitness or a stay at home mom that wants to get in shape, TPS offers professional and qualified personal fitness training sessions for all ages. We offer classes in speed and agility, core strength, body building, yoga and running groups. Our personal trainers will come to your home or you can schedule a class at one of our local public parks.

Fit and Sit

Introducing an innovative and exciting new program that gives parents an opportunity to work out with a personal trainer while watching their children participate in sports. Our certified personal trainers will come to your child’s sport location and give you a workout all while being able to watch your child participate in practice.

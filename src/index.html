About

Total Possession Soccer was founded in 2013 and has seen rapid growth since its birth. TPS offers a variety of soccer and fitness programs for kids and adults. What separates TPS from competing soccer training companies is that our coaches are passionate about the beautiful game and understand that in order to get youth athletes to reach their full potential, you must first find ways to connect with them individually and as team.

Classes

Team Training

TPS offers high energy and professional team training for town travel teams as well as recreation programs.

Game Day Coaching

Our professional coaches are all licensed and experienced in game day coaching for all ages and skill levels.

Private Lessons

TPS also offers one on one and group soccer and fitness training lessons for children and adults.

TPS ELITE

This six week summer program provides a fun and energetic atmosphere for our elite players to strive to compete at the top levels.
